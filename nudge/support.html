Nudge — Session Timer & Routines

Getting Started

  1. Open Nudge and choose a preset template or create a custom routine
  2. Add timed blocks to your routine (e.g. "Deep Work — 25 min", "Break — 5 min")
  3. From the Today tab, tap your routine and press Start
  4. Work through each block — when the timer expires, tap Next Block to advance
  5. Complete all blocks to finish your session and build your streak

Frequently Asked Questions

How do streaks work?

Complete at least one session per day to maintain your streak. Skipped days don't break your streak. If you miss a day, you can use a streak save (up to 7 per week) to keep it going.

What are Live Activities?

When a session is active, your current block, next block, and progress appear on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island. This works automatically — no setup needed.

How do I remove ads?

Purchase Nudge Pro ($1.99, one-time) from Settings. This removes all interstitial ads. Every feature is already free — Pro just removes the interruptions.

Can I restore my purchase on a new device?

Yes. Go to Settings and tap Restore Purchases. Your Nudge Pro purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

Is my data backed up?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you back up your iPhone via iCloud or Finder, your Nudge data is included in that backup.

Notifications aren't showing up.

Make sure notifications are enabled for Nudge in Settings > Notifications > Nudge. Also check that Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode isn't blocking them.
