Privacy Policy

Nudge — Daily Routine Reminders · Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Nudge is built with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device and is never collected, transmitted, or shared with anyone.

Data Collection

Nudge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-in is required
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used
  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data is sent to us or any third party

Data Storage

All app data — routines, nudge logs, streaks, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Notifications

Nudge uses local notifications to remind you of your scheduled tasks. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No push notification servers are involved.

Advertising

The free version of Nudge displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Upgrading to Nudge Pro removes all ads and any associated data collection by AdMob.

Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google AdMob Ads (free tier only) Device identifiers per Google's policy
Apple StoreKit In-app purchases Handled entirely by Apple

No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

Nudge does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of Nudge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at tkappsdev.contact@gmail.com.
